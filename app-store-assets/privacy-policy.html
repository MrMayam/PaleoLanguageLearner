Privacy Policy for Paleo Hebrew Learning

Last updated: December 27, 2024

Introduction

Paleo Hebrew Learning ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Learning progress and achievements (stored locally on your device)
  • Purchase information for premium features
  • Optional account information if you create an account

Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage analytics (anonymous and aggregated)
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • Performance data to improve app functionality

How We Use Information

  • Provide personalized learning experiences
  • Track learning progress and achievements
  • Process subscription and purchase transactions
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Payment processing through Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • Anonymous analytics to improve our services
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is designed for children and families. We take special care to protect children's privacy:

  • We do not collect personal information from children under 13
  • Learning progress is stored locally on the device
  • Parents can delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • No advertising or tracking for children
  • Parental controls available for premium features

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure payment processing through official app stores
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of optional data collection
  • Export your learning progress data

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Learning progress: Until you delete the app or request deletion
  • Purchase information: As required by tax and accounting laws
  • Support communications: For up to 2 years for service improvement

Third-Party Services

We use limited third-party services:

  • Apple App Store for payment processing
  • Anonymous analytics for app improvement
  • Crash reporting to fix technical issues

International Users

This app complies with international privacy laws including GDPR for European users and PIPEDA for Canadian users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email for account holders.
